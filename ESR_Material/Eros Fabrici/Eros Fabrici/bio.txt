Eros Fabrici is a Marie Curie Early Stage Researcher at the
Athena Research and Innovation Center and the Universitat
Politiecnica de Catalunya. He obtained his BSc in Computer
Science, Web, and Multimedia Technologies at the University
of Udine and his MSc in Data Science and Scientific
Computing at the University of Trieste, both located in Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy. 
His research interests span Data and Software Engineering, Algorithms, Data Structures and Privacy.
In his free time, he enjoys doing sports and music research.
